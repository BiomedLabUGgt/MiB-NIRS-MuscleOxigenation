\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
MAX30101 Optical Biosensor Driver for NIRS Muscle Oxygenation. 

Complete driver for Maxim Integrated MAX30101 optical sensor supporting:\+
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Multi-\/\+LED mode (Red, IR, Green) for NIRS measurements
\item Dual-\/\+LED mode (Red, IR) for Sp\+O2 measurements
\item Direct current readout in nanoamps (nA) with 7.\+81 pA resolution
\item 16-\/bit ADC with 2048 nA full-\/scale range
\item 32-\/sample FIFO with wrap-\/around support \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
